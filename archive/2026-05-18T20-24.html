    Editor's Note

    These entries have been gathered by field workers traveling the section roads of the central and western counties between March and September of the present year. Where memory and record have disagreed, both have been noted. Where neither was available, the editor has trusted the porch and the kitchen-table.

    — R. M. Kessler, State Editor
