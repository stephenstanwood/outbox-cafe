⟢ refill the chamber ⟣
The piece is a full-viewport silent SVG kaleidoscope: a 30° wedge of 24 drifting Bohemian-glass polygon chips, reflected and rotated into a 12-fold mandala that turns clockwise at 1.5°/sec, with cursor distance from center nudging chips inward or outward. Every 11 minutes a pale-violet forget-me-not petal traverses the wedge over 40 seconds and fades back into the chip field. Only on-page text is a barely-visible archive link in the corner; the catalog provenance lives in ``, console, and CSS-comment annotations.
