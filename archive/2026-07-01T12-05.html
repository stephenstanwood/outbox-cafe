Turn, and it sings

A little paper-strip music box — every punched hole plucks a tooth. Wind it slowly; the best part hides in the middle.

Hold the crank  ·  or grab the paper and drag it at your own pace

Tune No. 7 — “Morning on the Wire.” Cut your own strips; it plays best after supper. The rest of the workshop is on the shelf → Paper by the yard. Almond cookies, six to a bag, from the Good Wok two doors down.
