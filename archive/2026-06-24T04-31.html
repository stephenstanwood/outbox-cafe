    AMERICAN BOWLING CONGRESS

    OFFICIAL CERTIFICATION OF SANCTIONED HIGH SERIES

    This is to certify that on the sixth day of March, nineteen hundred seventy-five, at the Holiday Lanes establishment located at 4814 Western Avenue, in the city of Toledo, in the County of Lucas, in the State of Ohio, in regular sanctioned league play of the Thursday Night Pin Splitters League (ABC Sanction No. 47-2218), bowler

    MRS. MARLENE T. WIECZOREK

    did roll a three-game series of 247 — 232 — 224, total 703, hereby certified as a sanctioned women's high series and entered upon the permanent records of this organization.

    League Secretary of record: Mrs. Doreen Pszczolkowski.
    House Manager of record: Mr. Robert J. Holzemer.
