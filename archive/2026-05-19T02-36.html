Eleanor Hutchins · Sighting Logbook

Burlington, Vermont · Audubon Chapter №14 · 1954 — 1962

A working personal field journal. Eleanor keeps it for the Chapter, but the Chapter keeps it back — members read each other's pages and write in. Pull a card to see the full observation, the field sketch as described, and any letters that came back.
