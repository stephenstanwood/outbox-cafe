TUESDAY · MARCH 9, 1976 STUDIO B · 412 PEACH ST. BROADCAST: 6:00P – 10:30P

Welcome, neighbors. We've got a full evening for you — seven programs, every one of them put together by people who live within four miles of the studio. Vera taped her show on Saturday with three real apples and one wax one (her words). Wendell is bringing two new poets we've never had on air before. And the Channel 12 Talent Showcase has, I am told, a teenager doing card tricks for the camera that I personally cannot wait to see.
