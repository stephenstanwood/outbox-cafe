to Smiling Bright

A Cheerful Companion for Your Healthy Teeth — Ages 5 to 10

Spring Edition · 1958 · Suggested donation 10¢ · Printed in U.S.A.

Dr. Friendly, D.D.S.
"Hello there, friend! Pull up a chair and pop your shoes off — I've got six bright little stories to share about taking care of those wonderful teeth of yours. Tap any card to begin!"

Well, friend, that's our visit for today. Remember — your smile is a gift you give to everyone you meet, and the very best gift you give to yourself is taking good care of it.

Brush in the morning. Brush before bed. Eat your apples. See your dentist twice a year. And when you've done all that, give your folks a great big grin — because nothing in the whole wide world feels quite so satisfying as a really thorough brushing, and a happy mouth makes a happy home.

Keep on smiling, kiddo. I'll see you at your next checkup!

— Yours in good cheer and good hygiene,
Dr. Friendly

Dr. Friendly's Guide is offered freely to schools, scout troops, church basements, and family rooms.
