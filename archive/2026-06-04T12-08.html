— PROGRAMME —

ACT THE FIRST THE CHINESE LINKING RINGS Eight rings of polished brass — they pass through one another at the wave of a hand. As performed nightly by Mr. Halloran since 1922.
ACT THE SECOND THE MARVELS OF MENTAL TELEGRAPHY Miss Lurena Mae Crebbin, blindfolded, shall read three sealed envelopes drawn from the audience and answer the questions therein.
ACT THE THIRD THE GREAT ROPE LIBERATION Young Mr. Cletus Boldweather, bound in five honest knots by gentlemen of the audience, shall free himself in under four minutes.

Souvenir Tray
Sept. 21, 1934
After the show tonight Lurena Mae and I are going to walk down to the courthouse square and sit on the bench by the pump and split a bottle of tonic ourselves. This is not in the program because it is not for the audience, it is the part of the night we do for us. Cletus will be asleep in the truck by then. The pump has good cold water and the bottle is for after. If you are reading this slip you have been to enough of our shows that I do not mind you knowing what we do after the curtain comes down. — Otis.
