★ Channel 19 Etiquette ★

eleven rules, no more, no fewer. Doris says any more and you'd just be showing off.

  1. Say your handle when you key up. Mystery is for novels.
  2. If somebody's flagging help, you stop. End of sentence.
  3. Mama Hen gets the last word in a squabble. Doris seconds it.
  4. Smokey reports — be specific. "MP 117 eastbound, photo radar." Not "uhh somewhere by the thing."
  5. Weather chatter is a public service, not a brag. Keep it tight.
  6. No politics on 19. Take that nonsense to 22.
  7. Singing along to the AM is fine. Key up to sing it ON the channel — pick a different night.
  8. If you're new, say so. The boys will be sweet about it. (Mostly.)
  9. Don't talk over a lady downshifting on a grade. She's busy.
  10. Pie reports are sacred. Honest pie reports MORE so.
  11. 10-7 means 10-7. Sign off, then sign off. Don't pop back in for one more thing.
