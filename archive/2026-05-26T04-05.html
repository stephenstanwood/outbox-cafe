Each phrase becomes a small traveling voice. Walls catch it, throw it back, soften it with every collision. Layer phrases on top of phrases and they tangle in the air the way sound actually does in a real room. The cathedral holds a voice for a while. The bathroom is hard and bright. The canyon refuses to give anything back at first.
