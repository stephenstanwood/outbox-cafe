Manager's Nightly Logbook · Summer Season · 1957

Good evening, friend. Frank Czerny — owner-manager going on fourteen years now. Pull up a folding chair on the projection-booth steps. The bulbs are warming, the popcorn salt is loaded, and Hank just radioed that the first cars are turning off Cleveland Avenue. I keep the logbook handy because every night out here is its own kind of weather, and I want to remember. — Frank
