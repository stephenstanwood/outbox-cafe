Dial-A-Bulletin
Local No. 1204 · Pinehurst Div.
FORM 1204-B
MEMBER USE

Friends, if you'll step right over here — this instrument has served Local 1204 for over forty years. Each number carries a dispatch from the current posting. Take your time. The route is well-marked.

Dial any number to receive your dispatch. All notices current as of posting.

imagine: the sound this telephone makes. Brass-warm. A returning click and a rattle of the spring. You can hear the whole route in number 7 if you listen past the tone.
