i made a WORD LADDER!!!!! out of all my rocks!!! ★ NEW ★

posted by wendell · tuesday morning before school · 4 comments · filed in: ROCKS, PUZZLES, MY DAD

okay so!!! my dad is a engineer and he draws on this BLUE PAPER all the time with white pencil. its called blueprint paper and it is for buildings. but i asked if i could have some for my rocks and he said YES so now this is the blue paper version of my blog!!! (the regular one is on blogger but the colors got weird today.)

OKAY so here is the BIG NEWS. my pack of pet rocks?? all of their names make a WORD LADDER. i didnt even MEAN to do that. it just HAPPENED!!! i was lining them up to count them again and i saw it. EACH ROCK'S NAME IS ONE LETTER DIFFERENT FROM THE ROCK NEXT TO IT. isnt that incredibe??? my mom says incredible is spelled like that.

here is the ladder. you can click on a rock's name and it will show you what letter is the new letter, AND it will scroll you down to that rocks personel card. try it!!!

ROCK the original. found him first.
LOCK guards the back gate.
LICK tongue-shape. NOT lickable.
LACK missing a chip. still cool.
BACK has a big hump on top.
PACK that's ALL of them together!!

tap a row → the new letter lights up & it shows you that rock

START: ROCK · END: PACK · five hops · my dad helped me check that they are all real words and he said YES.
