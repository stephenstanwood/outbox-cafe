To the Citizen Observer:

Every report filed in this index was submitted by an American observer who took the time to write down what they saw. Some accounts have been resolved. Many have not. The Air Force regards each filed report as a serious scientific contribution and treats every witness as a credible reporter of their own experience until shown otherwise.

Browse the index below. Click any card to open the full incident form. Use the filter rows to narrow by year, region, observed shape, or current case classification. If you yourself have observed an unexplained aerial event, please complete the citizen report form at the bottom of this index.

— Lt. Col. R. Kingsley · Special Projects Liaison · drafted spring 1954
