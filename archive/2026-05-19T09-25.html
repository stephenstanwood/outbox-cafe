Inner Compass · Vol. IV, No. 3 · Autumn 1972

Your Cosmic Type

Eight quiet questions toward your truest, most-aligned self.

Prepared by the Hollybrook Center for Aligned Living · Mendocino, CA

Dear seeker — what follows is not a parlor game. It is a real instrument, refined across many evenings here at the Hollybrook Center, and it is here for you. Each preference you hold — a color, a season, a small ordinary food — is a thread the cosmos has been weaving into you since the morning of your birth. Answer truthfully and your archetype will arrive. Take your time. The scoring bar at the top of the page is listening as you go, and you may watch your truest self emerge while you decide.

Your Cosmic Alignment, in Progress

Answer a question — the bar will begin to speak.
