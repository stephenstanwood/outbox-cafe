On the West Pier · Sixpence the Cup

Vi Tupp Reads the Leaves

Behind the strung-bead curtain, third stall past the whelks
Sit yourself down, duck — there's a chair. Now sip it right down to the leaves: click the cup. Mind, it's hot off the kettle.
Vi reckons —
more cups left on the shelf
No refunds, no curses, no rude ones. Tea by the urn, leaves by the Almighty.
