Hello travelers! This is the country's friendliest swap club for genuine motel postcards. Drop us a card from your trip — we're putting the whole summer up on the corkboard. Air-cooled rooms, swimming pools, and the very best in roadside design from coast to coast.

What a season this is shaping up to be. Brand-new motor inns are opening every week from Tucumcari to Tampa, and every one of them has a postcard to be proud of. Click a card to flip it over and read what folks are sending home. Drag the ones you love into your Favorites — the soda counter prints up the top picks each Friday.
