ROUTE SEVEN · NIGHT LEDGER

H. Renn, lamplighter · Fourth Ward · December, 1925

Forty-one standards on the route. The route is walked twice — once at dusk to light, once before dawn to extinguish. The book stays in the satchel between. These are the pages of one month.
