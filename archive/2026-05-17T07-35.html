the kite pact!!! every spring, every kite anywhere agrees to LIFT, & the wind agrees to LIFT BACK, & this happens millions of times simultaneously across many many fields & rooftops & small hilly parks & this is, in our completely unbiased opinion, the most beautiful contract ever ratified in any jurisdiction!!! the wind has never broken the pact!!! not once!!! sometimes the wind takes a small pause & the kite settles & then the wind says "sorry, sorry, sorry!!!" & lifts again!!! it is so wholesome!!! we are honestly tearing up just writing about it!!!

several kites have asked to be specifically named in this caption. due to space limitations we honor only the eldest: HONEYBEE, a yellow box kite from 1948, last seen over the great salt flats & presumed still airborne!!! we believe in you, honeybee!!!

APPENDIX & FREQUENTLY POSED QUESTIONS!!!

is the wind one (1) entity or many entities?
YES!!! both!!! all the answers to this question are simultaneously correct!!! the wind contains plural pronouns & singular pronouns & sometimes pronouns we have not invented yet!!! we use "she/it/they/the wind" interchangeably with the wind's full enthusiastic consent!!!
did the wind really receive its first fan letter in 1492?
the FIRST PRESERVED fan letter is from 1492!!! the wind has assured us that earlier letters absolutely existed but were, in many cases, simply addressed verbally & then carried off in the wind itself, which is a recursive complication we are still untangling!!!
can i submit a story about the wind?
YES!!! the curatorial team accepts submissions in all the following formats: handwritten note, whispered aloud at any window, hummed into a kettle, pressed into a leaf & left under a stone!!! we will receive it eventually!!! the wind is helping us with delivery!!!
is the wind affiliated with any major weather services?
NO!!! the wind is fully independent!!! the wind has been invited to consult with several & declines every offer politely with a small playful gust through their office curtains!!!
does the wind ever rest?
not in any way YOU would call resting!!! the wind sometimes goes very still & we used to think this was rest, but the wind has clarified: this is the wind LISTENING!!! the wind is the best listener currently active in any sphere!!!
