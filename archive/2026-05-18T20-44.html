A Personal Word from the Director of Enrollments

Friend — and I shall call you friend, for any soul who opens this catalog has already begun the work — there has never been a finer season in the history of the world to better oneself. The mails run swift. The lamps burn bright. A man or woman of ordinary means may today receive, by post, the exact instruction once reserved for the sons of bankers and the daughters of magistrates. We have seen it ten thousand times. We will see it again with you.

Every course on these pages has been refined across two decades of practical correspondence. Our instructors read every lesson you mail us, by hand, and return their notes within nine days. Your tools, books, and certificates of completion are included in the tuition — there are no hidden charges of any kind. If, after one full month of study, you find the course is not for you, post your materials back and we return your money entire. We have always done so. We always shall.

The ambition that brought you to this page is not an accident. Honor it. Enroll.

Florence H. Hollings Director of Enrollments · Scranton, Pa.
